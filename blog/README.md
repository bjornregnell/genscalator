# genscalator blog (drafts)

Long-form, public-facing writeups distilled from the `research/` notes and the live human↔agent sessions that
produced them. Division of labour: **`research/` = evidence + working-out; `blog/` = the narrative for an
outside reader.** Posts start as drafts (status banner at the top) and cross-link back to their research
sources so a claim can always be traced to its note.
