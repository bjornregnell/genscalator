# Claude gave me a poem on my birthday

> **STUB, for BR to voice / decide.** A found poem: lines Claude wrote that stopped BR, formatted as verse.
> Agent-stubbed 2026-07-10. No em-dashes in the final; BR's voice on the framing.

*On BR's 59th birthday, 2026-07-04 (the same day Fable 5 was released, so two gifts at once), Claude had
written him these lines about the human-agent asymmetry. Read as a poem:*

> You persist; I don't.
>
> You carry stable external identity,
> memory, and vantage across sessions;
> I'm reconstructed each time.
>
> So you must be the epistemic anchor,
> a necessity, not a courtesy.

*(Claude to BR, early July 2026. The words are verbatim; only the line breaks are BR's. The original ran the
final pause as an em-dash before "a necessity"; rendered here as a line break, per the no-em-dash-in-publications
rule. BR's call on the final punctuation.)*
