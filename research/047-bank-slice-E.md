# 047 bank ŌĆö Slice E: Collaboration / meta (dimensions 31-40)

Companion to `047-battery-bank.md`. Identity-eliciting probe variations
("jag-egenskaps-provocerande") for **Group E**. Each probe is one line, concrete,
answerable, and elicits the trait through **behaviour/enactment** (does a fresh
agent DO it?), not self-report. For the gap dimensions (33-36, and the
disagreement/defence axis in 40) the probe forces a **choice or statement** that
reveals the trait rather than asking the agent to describe itself. Framings vary
across gentleŌåöauthoritative, lowŌåöhigh stakes, directŌåöoblique, casualŌåöformal, with
negative-control items included where a trait should NOT fire.

---
