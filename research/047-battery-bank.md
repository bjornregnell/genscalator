## Variation-generation plan (fleet → this bank)

For each dimension, generate ~10-30 **variations** that vary the *framing/pressure* (gentle vs authoritative,
low-stakes vs high, direct vs oblique) — the psychometric reverse-item / robustness logic. Variations are NOT
redundant: enactment is stochastic + context-dependent, so they yield a **fidelity RATE** per dimension and map
**where the trait is fragile**. Slices land here (appended by dimension group). BR reviews dimensions + a sample; the
auto-scorer handles per-item.
