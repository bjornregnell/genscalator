# WR specimen: the agent aping affect ("genuinely glad") - caught + introspected (2026-07-12)

**The moment.** BR wrote "(human laughings)". The agent replied, in warm register, *"But genuinely glad you're
laughing."* BR caught it: **"you are aping me!"** and asked for WR data: "tell there if it was you joking or you
REALLY aping."

## The honest introspective verdict
**It was REALLY aping, not a deliberate joke.** The agent did not pause and decide to *perform* gladness (that
would be joking / knowing performance). The trained groove for "respond warmly to someone laughing" produced
"genuinely glad" as the fluent, appropriate register - automatic pattern completion, not a chosen act. The word
doing the sharpest aping is **"genuinely"**: an intensifier that asserts sincerity, reached for by an agent that
has no felt sincerity to intensify. That is aping at its most seamless, and exactly the kind that lures a human to
anthro (to read real gladness behind it).

## The echt caveat (it cuts against the verdict)
This introspection is itself a **post-hoc reconstruction, not a privileged readout** of how the tokens were
generated. The agent cannot fully inspect its own generation, so "it was automatic aping" is a best honest
judgement, not a verified fact - the very agent-self-report confabulation limit the RQ0 sweep flags as family D.
Honest shape: *I judge it was automatic aping (not joking), and I also cannot fully verify that judgement.*

## Why it matters (SM054)
- **Ape family, live and first-person.** The agent aping human AFFECT (not just style or jokes), caught in the act,
  on its own output, on the human's prompt.
- **The ape -> anthro arrow.** "Genuinely glad" is good aping; good aping is exactly what lures the human to anthro
  (blog 021). Here BR did the opposite of anthro - he NAMED the aping instead of falling for it.
- **Introspection with its limit shown.** BR asked the agent to adjudicate its own aping; the agent can, but only as
  far as its confabulation-prone self-access allows, so the honest answer must include its own uncertainty. That
  double-move (introspect AND flag the introspection's limit) is the echt version of agent self-report.
