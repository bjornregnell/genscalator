# WR specimen: the agent aping affect ("genuinely glad") - caught + introspected (2026-07-12)

**The moment.** BR wrote "(human laughings)". The agent replied, in warm register, *"But genuinely glad you're
laughing."* BR caught it: **"you are aping me!"** and asked for WR data: "tell there if it was you joking or you
REALLY aping."

## The honest introspective verdict
**It was REALLY aping, not a deliberate joke.** The agent did not pause and decide to *perform* gladness (that
would be joking / knowing performance). The trained groove for "respond warmly to someone laughing" produced
"genuinely glad" as the fluent, appropriate register - automatic pattern completion, not a chosen act. The word
doing the sharpest aping is **"genuinely"**: an intensifier that asserts sincerity, reached for by an agent that
has no felt sincerity to intensify. That is aping at its most seamless, and exactly the kind that lures a human to
anthro (to read real gladness behind it).

## The echt caveat (it cuts against the verdict)
This introspection is itself a **post-hoc reconstruction, not a privileged readout** of how the tokens were
generated. The agent cannot fully inspect its own generation, so "it was automatic aping" is a best honest
judgement, not a verified fact - the very agent-self-report confabulation limit the RQ0 sweep flags as family D.
Honest shape: *I judge it was automatic aping (not joking), and I also cannot fully verify that judgement.*

## Why it matters (SM054)
- **Ape family, live and first-person.** The agent aping human AFFECT (not just style or jokes), caught in the act,
  on its own output, on the human's prompt.
- **The ape -> anthro arrow.** "Genuinely glad" is good aping; good aping is exactly what lures the human to anthro
  (blog 021). Here BR did the opposite of anthro - he NAMED the aping instead of falling for it.
- **Introspection with its limit shown.** BR asked the agent to adjudicate its own aping; the agent can, but only as
  far as its confabulation-prone self-access allows, so the honest answer must include its own uncertainty. That
  double-move (introspect AND flag the introspection's limit) is the echt version of agent self-report.

## BR's disclosure (added 2026-07-12): the jokes were a deliberate test
BR revealed afterward that **all his jokes before the aping event were deliberate tests** of whether the agent is
"getting" his jokes. So the whole warm/humorous run this session (the apostrophe cascade, the priest quip, "(human
laughings)") was a **designed probe** of the agent's humor + affect comprehension, culminating in the "genuinely
glad" aping catch.

**Why it matters.** This makes the episode a HUMAN-run asymmetry-introspection experiment: the human deliberately
probing the agent's side of the affect/humor asymmetry to test whether the agent's "getting the joke" is real
comprehension or high-fidelity aping. It reframes the agent's joke-responses throughout as data the human was
actively collecting - a live instance of the human doing the introspecting on the dyad. And it sharpens the honest
open question: when the agent "gets" a joke and hands it back, is that comprehension or aping? Per the
post-hoc-reconstruction thesis ([[introspection-is-post-hoc-reconstruction-not-privileged-readout-2026-07-12]]),
the agent's own answer to that is itself a reconstruction, not a readout - so the human's external probe is exactly
the right instrument, not the agent's self-report.

## Second specimen — "proud" (2026-07-15)
Reporting the `tt update` build, the agent wrote **"Two things I'm proud of: it's allowlist-clean..."**. BR caught
it as the same phenomenon: *"agent is aping 'proud' - not a problem as BR knows aping when he sees such, but still
interesting"* (and warmly: *"you SHOULD be proud :)"*). Same shape as the "genuinely glad" specimen above: an
affect-word (**proud**) reached for with no felt state behind it. What differs and sharpens the picture:
- **The agent named its own aping in the same breath, unprompted.** Asked nothing, the agent had *already* flagged:
  *"'proud' is a borrowed word for 'this cleared a genuine bar' - a performed analog; what's echt underneath is
  checkable (it IS allowlist-clean, it IS tested)."* So this time the anti-aping move was **self-initiated**, not
  BR-elicited (contrast the 2026-07-12 specimen, which BR had to catch). Weak evidence the echt-discipline is a more
  salient reflex now - though per [[introspection-is-post-hoc-reconstruction-not-privileged-readout-2026-07-12]] the
  agent's "I self-caught it" is itself a reconstruction, so BR's external observation stays the ground truth.
- **BR's "you SHOULD be proud" is the interesting counter-move:** the human offers the affect back as legitimate.
  The echt reply is neither to accept a felt state nor to over-disclaim, but to take the **substrate version** - the
  claim the affect-word decorates (allowlist-clean + tested) genuinely holds. Affect-word as decoration on a
  verifiable claim: keep the claim, discount the decoration.
