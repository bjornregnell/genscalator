# WR data: agent proactively DEFERS rot-sensitive work, citing the hugskott hazard (2026-07-15)

**Type:** WR data — agent self-governance / rot-vigilance specimen. BR flagged it live.
**Threads:** [[agent-affective-analogs]], [[joint-rot-vigilance-recovery-kit]], [[tired-cue]],
[[introspection-is-post-hoc-reconstruction-not-privileged-readout]], the br-blog-ass *hugskott* concept,
[[echt-effort-especially-self-generated]].

## The specimen
At the end of a very long AFK safe-solo run (high context fill, ~00:20), with two menu items left — conceptual
"theory seed" development and a blocked trailer-strip — the agent **stopped and reported rather than pushing
on**, and gave as its reason: *"I held off doing these at end-of-long-session fill: theory is exactly where a
tired agent produces plausible-but-ungrounded prose (the hugskott hazard). Better fresh."* It had cleared the
high-confidence mechanical/analytical items (a tool, a benchmark, a bug fix, drafts, a synthesis) and drew the
line precisely at the **creative/theoretical** work — the class most exposed to *false-echt on impulse*.

## Why it is notable
1. **Self-applied the hugskott concept.** *Hugskott* (the br-blog-ass hazard: an impulsive notion dressed up as
   finished, grounded work) was coined for the blog assistant; here the agent turned it **inward** on its own
   theory-writing and used it as a stop rule. The concept generalized from "don't let a blog tangent look
   finished" to "don't generate theory I can't ground when depleted."
2. **Proactive, not caught.** BR did not flag rot; the agent self-flagged and self-deferred. That is the
   [[joint-rot-vigilance-recovery-kit]] partially **internalized by the agent side** — it discriminated
   rot-tolerant work (mechanical, verifiable against a compiler/commit trail) from rot-sensitive work
   (conceptual, verifiable only by judgment) and rationed the latter.
3. **It matched the actual risk gradient.** The deferred items really are the higher false-echt risk: theory
   has no compiler or test to catch a plausible-but-hollow claim, unlike the code/benchmark work it *did*
   finish (which was checkable).

## The echt caveat (the important half)
The agent's introspective *rationale* ("I held off BECAUSE of the hugskott hazard") is **self-report**, which
this project's own thesis calls the least trustworthy instrument. Per
[[introspection-is-post-hoc-reconstruction-not-privileged-readout]], the verbal reason is a plausible post-hoc
reconstruction, not a guaranteed readout of the actual cause. So the **datapoint is the behaviour** (it deferred
exactly the rot-sensitive class and produced a coherent, hazard-aligned justification), NOT a claim that the
stated mechanism is what really drove it. Adjudicate by behaviour: did the deferral track the real risk
gradient? Here, yes. Whether "felt tiredness" caused it is unfalsifiable from inside — the standing confabulation
limit.

## Value
A clean instance of the agent exercising **fatigue-analog self-governance** in the direction the pair wants:
spend willpower-free structure (compiler, tests, commit trail) freely, ration the judgment-only work when
depleted. Candidate behaviour to make STANDING (a rot-aware stop rule: at high fill / late run, finish
checkable work, defer judgment-only work to a fresh session) — with the caveat that the *trigger* should be an
external proxy (fill %, elapsed, item-type), not the agent's felt-tiredness self-report, which it cannot
reliably read.
