## The artefact
In ~30 minutes of rapid co-design the dyad produced the **unified `?`-mode grammar** (SM118) + a **measurable-proxy
status-line design grounded in the actual Claude Code statusline JSON schema** (SM117): every mode carries a `?` when
inferred from a proxy over a configurable threshold; the `?` clears on confirmation; and — the sharp part — the
**confirmation is asymmetric** (the human self-confirms their own interior via a cue like `:Z`; the agent cannot
self-confirm its own state and needs external evidence), which makes the mode line wear the affective-channel /
introspection-is-reconstruction findings *by construction* rather than hiding them.

## Why it is a COUPLED capability (the division of labour)
Neither party would have produced this alone with reasonable effort:
- **BR supplied high-velocity generative ideation** — token-count as the rot gauge, char-count as the fatigue gauge,
  `tired?`, the `?`-uncertainty grammar, `:Z`-clears-the-`?`, the `dumb-zone?` extension, the typo/off-topic proxies.
  These arrived faster than either the agent or a lone human working carefully would generate them.
- **The agent + a minion supplied rapid grounding + synthesis** the human "could not do himself with reasonable
  effort": the claude-code-guide minion dug the CC docs and the statusline stdin-JSON schema to *confirm*
  `transcript_path` exists and enumerate the token/duration fields; the agent verified the transcript actually holds
  the data (per-turn `output_tokens`, user turns), surfaced the confirmation-asymmetry refinement, added the echt
  caveats (cumulative vs current-context tokens; typo-count only measurable in sent text; wall-clock is a noisy
  thinking proxy), tied each idea to the existing research threads, and captured it all durably in pins in real time.
- **The loop is the mechanism:** a tight **generate → ground → synthesise → capture** cycle running at conversational
  speed — BR spawns an idea, the agent grounds + echt-checks + links it, the minion parallel-fetches external truth,
  the pin banks it — so momentum never has to stop for doc-spelunking or note-taking. That loop is the reusable
  finding, more than any one idea in it.

## Echt framing of the claims (keep the surface honest)
- **"Truly novel"** is BR's hedge ("*probably*") and should stay hedged: novel *to us / not seen by us*, plausibly
  novel — global novelty is unverifiable from here, so do not assert it. The `?`-grammar's specific
  confirmation-asymmetry formulation is what feels new.
- **The VERIFIABLE claim is the coupled-capability one:** the artefact exists, it was produced in ~30 min, and BR
  (the authority on his own counterfactual) credibly reports he could not have done the doc/JSON grounding solo with
  reasonable effort. That is the echt datapoint; "novel" rides on top of it, hedged.
- Guard the mirror: this note is the *dyad* observing its own emergent capability. BR flagged it (external
  observation), which is the right instrument; the agent's own "we did something great" would be the affect-aping
  failure mode — so the load-bearing claim rests on BR's counterfactual report, not the agent's enthusiasm.

## Why it matters / next
- It is a concrete instance of genscalator's core productivity thesis: **the dyad > either party**, with a nameable
  mechanism (the generate→ground→synthesise→capture loop) and a nameable enabler (cheap minion doc-grounding that
  removes the human's spelunking cost). Candidate **blog case study** (coupled capability, grounded in this episode).
- Testable-ish: the counterfactual ("could BR have reached SM117/SM118 solo with reasonable effort?") is a member
