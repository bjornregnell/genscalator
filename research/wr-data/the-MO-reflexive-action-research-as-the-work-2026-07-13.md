# WR data / METHODOLOGY candidate: the MO — reflexive action research done AS the work (2026-07-13)

BR: *"generalize what we just did to an abstract meta level ... I think this is an important modus operandi of
joint research work."* This note names it. **Candidate to fold into `research/METHODOLOGY.md`.**

## The core abstraction
We do not do "work, then research about the work." The work and the study of the work are **one activity**: a
tight **doing ↔ studying loop** in which the live joint engagement is, at once, the **product** (the shipped
thing), the **instrument** (the probe that surfaces findings), and the **data** (the specimen we mine). Research
is not a phase; it is a **stance held continuously while doing**.

## The loop (one turn of it)
1. **DO** a real task (compact MEMORY.md, ship the mode line, move a skill, fix a colour).
2. Something **SURFACES** as a byproduct — a friction (a guard trip), a slip (the near-leak), a harness
   behaviour (the memory read-limit), a distinction (two "compactions"), even a *phrasing* ("almost nothing,
   the way I did it").
3. One of us **FLAGS** it — "WR data", a question, a hypothesis — promoting the incidental to an object of study.
4. We **REFLECT** — disambiguate, ask "what does this generalise to?", "is there a risk?", "does the hypothesis
   hold?".
5. We **CAPTURE** durably — a WR note, a memory, a pin, an SM, a dance, a cue.
6. We **ESCALATE** to an artifact — a tool, a skill, a checklist — and often set up a *future empirical* test
   (efficiency / effectiveness).
7. **Repeat.** The doing keeps generating studiable byproducts; the studying keeps feeding the doing.

## What makes it work (structural features)
- **The work IS the data.** No separate experiment; the real task, done for real, is the specimen — cheap,
  echt, unlimited supply. (RT047: the whole engagement is its data.)
- **Real-time reflexivity.** We reflect *while* doing, not after — and the agent's own **narration is a probe**:
  "I'm compacting…" surfaced the harness read-limit; the agent's phrasing surfaced method-contingency.
- **Complementary vantages = genuinely JOINT.** The human notices what the agent cannot (its own state, the
  stale chrome, the near-repercussion); the agent surfaces what the human cannot (harness internals, its own
  pseudocode). **Neither vantage alone yields the finding.** The observability asymmetry, turned into a method.
- **Continuous externalisation.** Capture is not overhead — it is the mechanism that turns ephemeral doing into
  cumulative knowledge, *because neither party can hold it all* (the human overflows his queue; the agent loses
  it at compaction). The substrate is the lab notebook.
- **The incidental becomes the object.** Frictions, slips, quirks, phrasings — the mundane byproducts — ARE the
  findings. The papercut is the datum.
- **An escalation ladder.** observation → named pin → hypothesis → artifact (skill/dance/tool) → *future*
  empirical evaluation. Description → design → evaluation: **engineering research (describe → improve →
  evaluate) run continuously, in miniature.**
- **The reflexive triple-role of the agent.** The agent is **instrument, object, AND co-researcher** at once —
  it executes, it is studied, and it introspects/theorises. Load-bearing caveat (family E): **self-report ≠
  self-certification**; behavioural proof + the human's external read adjudicate. This triple-role is the
  genuinely novel bit.
- **Conceptual hygiene as method.** Splitting an overloaded word (compaction: harness-context vs
  agent-authored-file) yields the insight. Naming + disambiguating IS a research act.

## Why it's powerful / what's novel
- It **collapses the doing/studying gap** — no lost time, no artificial setups; the flow of work IS the flow of
  findings (this is what `hot-harvest` mode marks).
- It is **action research where one researcher is also the instrument being studied** — a reflexive fold classic
  action research lacks (the tool can report its own procedure, introspect its own reasoning, *and be wrong
  about both*).
- It **self-documents**: the same substrate that runs the work (PB, memories, git) is the research record. The
  method leaves its own audit trail.
- It **compounds**: each finding becomes a cue / dance / skill that improves the next round of doing — the
  studying literally upgrades the instrument.

## The honest limit (the method must hold itself to its own standard)
The reflexivity is also the risk: the agent theorising its own behaviour is unfalsifiable from inside (family
E), and the human is a *participant*, not a neutral observer (caffeinated, carried-away). So the method needs
its guards — **behavioural proof over introspective say-so**, the human's external read, the echt discipline,
and *not over-claiming* (the "almost nothing, **the way I did it**" honesty). The method studies itself, so it
must meet its own echt bar.
